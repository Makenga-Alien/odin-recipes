Stir-Fly Recipe

Description

A quick and vibrant chicken stir-fry with colorful vegetables, perfect for a healthy and flavorful weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast, cubed
  • Broccoli florets
  • Bell peppers, sliced
  • Carrot slices
  • Onion, sliced
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger, minced
  • Garlic, minced
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Cook chicken until browned.
  2. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp
  3. Add soy sauce, ginger, and garlic; cook briefly.
  4. Serve hot.
