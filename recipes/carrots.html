Carrots Recipe

Description

These carrots are roasted with a sweet and spicy glaze made with honey and chili flakes.

They're perfect as a side dish for any meal, and they're easy to make!

The vibrant colors and bold flavors will make them a standout on your dinner table.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Toss carrots with olive oil, honey, chili flakes, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread carrots in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  4. Roast for 20-25 minutes, or until tender and slightly caramelized.
