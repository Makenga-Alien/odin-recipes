Chicken Recipe

Description

Crispy baked chicken with herbs and lemon, a simple and flavorful dish. Perfect for a family dinner, easy to prepare with minimal ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Chicken drumsticks
  • Lemon, sliced
  • Garlic cloves
  • Fresh herbs (rosemary, thyme)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss chicken with olive oil, herbs, salt, and pepper.
  3. Arrange chicken, lemon slices, and garlic on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until chicken is cooked through.
